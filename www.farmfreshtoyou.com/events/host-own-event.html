Host Your Own Event

Nestled in the beautiful Capay Valley, visitors are welcomed to the farm by picturesque, rolling hills and vivid sunsets. Events are held along an oak-tree and lavender-lined, grassy plateau overlooking fields of fruits and vegetables and nearby citrus and stone fruit orchards.

Since starting our organic farm in 1976, we've welcomed school visits, business team building retreats, farm tours, community events and wedding celebrations to the land. Over the years, it's been an honor to share our family farm with visitors and their families, friends and communities.

With multiple options for event and ceremony locations, our farm works well for both small gatherings and large celebrations of up to 400 guests. Our farm is located 90 minutes northeast of San Francisco or 30 minutes northwest of Sacramento.
