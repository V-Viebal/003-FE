OUR MISSION

Our mission is to connect people with the farmers and land that grows their food by offering farm tours from May through October, and Farmer & Chef events through the year.

FUN FOR A GOOD CAUSE

*These special events benefit the Kathleen Barsotti Non-Profit for Sustainable Agriculture and/or local food banks.

 

Event Season starts back up soon!
Look forward to...

FARM TOURS

Harvest something fresh from the field, ride the tractor tram and enjoy a scenic view of the hills, fields and orchards. Join in on a kids activity, listen to a farm talk, visit our market stand, and more! We host tours May through October.

FARM FESTIVALS*

Enjoy farm-style fun with live music and dancing, delicious local food and beverages, camping in the orchards and additional farm activities for the whole family!

FARMER & CHEF SEASONAL DINNERS*

Sit down to a farm-to-fork dinner celebrating the season's harvest with your farmer and chef. Our next dinner is this winter and makes a unique holiday gift idea. Look for details and tickets in early December.

 

Have Your Event on Our Farm!

Since starting our organic farm in 1976, we’ve welcomed school visits, team retreats, farm tours, community events and wedding celebrations to the land. Learn more about hosting your next event on our beautiful farm.
